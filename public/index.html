What is this website about?

Our mission

The COVID-19 pandemic, also known as the coronavirus pandemic, is an ongoing global pandemic of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). The virus was first identified in December 2019 in Wuhan, China.

We created this website to help you to keep track of visited location. You can see the current hotspots in the map to avoid the areas that are dangerous. Furthermore, you can keep track of the visited locations by entering the longitude and latitude of the location, therefore, you can check whether you have been to a hotspot!
